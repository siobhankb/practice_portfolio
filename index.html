Sometimes Winners Quit

When the thing you love doesn't love you back

I taught for over 15 years.
I am a National Board Certified Teacher in Early and Middle Childhood Literacy.
I have taught in public, charter, and private schools.
The pandemic broke me.
So I quit. I moved to a rural town, filled with white people and bland food because I loved the school. I bought a house a few blocks away because I thought I'd retire from that school. The plan was never to leave teaching...
But here I am. Demoralized, disillusioned, yet determined to learn and to lead the way for others.

Teachers 2 Techies

Despite some teachers' bad tech reputations, we make awesome tech workers

Think about it: Teachers are people who make more decisions in a day than emergency room physicians. We have to creatively come up with ways to entertain, care for, inspire, connect with, and instruct 25-35 young people all at the same time, often at the last minute because prep got canceled or it's a rainy-day-recess. Teachers are flexible thinkers, problem-solvers, project-managers, user-experience-designers, and so much more! Plus, we're fun people. You should really #HireFormerTeachers!!!
